```flow
st=>start: 开始
op1=>operation: 读入秘密K
op2=>operation: 生成数组d[n]
cond=>condition: 判断是否满足N>K>M
op3=>operation: K依次模d生成密钥串k[n]
op4=>operation: 使用t个密钥利用CRT还原K'
cond2=>condition: 判断K'是否等于K
e1=>end: 恢复成功
e2=>end: 恢复失败

st->op1->op2->cond->op3->op4->cond2
cond(yes)->op3
cond(no)->op2
cond2(yes)->e1
cond2(no)->e2
```
